heynow dance

summer 2011

  • home | winter | autumn

in summer 2011 heynow did not quite exist. there was time spent car chatting about making a dance show that was interesting and comfortable for non-dancers and engaging for serious artists. there was notebook scribbling, planning pieces considering the difference between a stage and 'normal' space. and there was some communication with friends known to be inventive and flexible dance and music artists. then we picked up some food and cheap wine and made a show!
